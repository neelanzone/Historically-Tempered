About Us

Welcome to "Thali by Thali". This project was born out of a desire to document the diverse and rich culinary heritage of our region. A "Thali" is more than just a plate; it is a balanced meal, a cultural symbol, and a story on a platter. Our team has traveled extensively to capture these stories, bringing you 15 unique episodes that explore the nuance of flavor and tradition.
